ATM & Digital Banking

Article

ATMs (Automated Teller Machines) allow you to withdraw cash, check your balance, and sometimes even deposit money without going inside the bank. To use an ATM, you need your ATM card and your secret PIN (Personal Identification Number). Always keep your PIN private and never share it with anyone.

To withdraw money from an ATM: Insert your card, enter your PIN, select "Withdrawal," choose "Savings" or "Current" account, enter the amount, and collect your cash and receipt. Always count your cash before leaving the ATM.

Digital banking refers to using mobile apps or internet banking for financial transactions. UPI (Unified Payments Interface) is a popular digital payment system in India that allows instant money transfers directly from your bank account using a mobile app. Other methods include mobile wallets and online bill payments. Digital banking is convenient and fast, but always be careful about scams and only use trusted apps and websites. Never share your OTP (One Time Password) or PIN with anyone.
