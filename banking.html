Understanding the Banking Process

Article

Banking is a process that helps people store, save, and manage their money safely. You can open a bank account by visiting a nearby bank with your Aadhaar card and photo. Once your account is active, you will get an ATM card and can use it to withdraw cash anytime...

Banks also help you get loans, save money in fixed deposits, and use mobile apps to send or receive money. In rural areas, banks also work through Business Correspondents (Bank Mitras) who help people access services.
